INGREDIENTS: 

2 tbls Olive Oil
2 cloves diced garlic
4 boneless chicken breasts
pinch salt
pinch pepper

DIRECTIONS:

In a medium saucepan, heat oil on med-high.
Add garlic
Saute garlic until lightly brown
Add salt, pepper
Add chicken breasts and saute for about 5 minutes
Turn breasts and saute for about 3 mins or until chicken is cooked through
